Our Purpose

The Borbonus
Family Foundation

The Borbonus Family Foundation is dedicated to honoring the ultimate sacrifice of Army Specialist John Borbonus, a hero from the 82nd Airborne Division who saved countless lives. Our mission is to preserve his legacy of courage, leadership, and selfless service by giving back to the community he loved.

Through dedicated community initiatives, support for veterans, and continuous outreach, we strive to build a stronger Idaho and ensure that the bravery of our fallen heroes is never forgotten.
